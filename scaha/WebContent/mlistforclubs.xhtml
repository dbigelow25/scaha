Downloading your Club's Master List
getting a snapshot of your currently registered players
Click any icon to download your master club list in various formats. Each Icon will download it in the suggested format.

This list contains all the information you need to build a contact list for every team. In addition, It contains the USA Hockey Numbers for you. You can basically take the first two columns and import them right into Cyber Sport and bypass any/all scanning and manual entry.

Currently.. it downloads last year's data. Once we start the 2014/15 Tryouts.. we will switch this over to the current season so you can start collecting information on your teams.

Once we get through tryouts, we will be able to possibly create one master Excel Workbook and have one team per tab. For now though, give it a shot and see what you can do with the data once its downloaded into an XL File.
