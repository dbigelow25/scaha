This year, SCAHA is continuing to use an entirely ONLINE LOI process.   In order to participate in SCAHA this year, you will need to perform two tasks.
 
  1. Register for USA Hockey Membership NOW for the 2015/16 season
  2. Register each player with SCAHA and receive a 5 digit Season Pass Code.
 
Both of these things need to be done prior to attending tryouts this year.   So its important that you take care of this now.
 
To get things started, you will use the same SCAHA account created last year. You can log onto your account by visiting http://www.scaha.com to do the following:

  1. Manage your profile (pretty self-explanatory). 
  2. Register your family  members for the upcoming SCAHA season.
 
We ask that you log in and double check the profile for you and make any changes to address, phone, e-mail, or password, etc...
After you have dialed that in, we want you to focus on the second thing at your disposal.. which is registering family members for the upcoming season.  This can also be you if you plan on being a Manager, Volunteer, or Coach.
 
Now.. we need you to perform the following steps to complete both the USA Hockey and SCAHA membership registration for this season’
 
  1. Go to USA Hockey (https://www.usahockeyregistration.com/login_input.action) and register your player(s) for the upcoming season.
  2. Make sure you have a copy of the 14 digit number that is generated by USAHockey - you will need this below.
  3. Go to (http://www.scaha.com) , log in.. and select Register for SCAHA in the "My Actions" menu
  4. You will see a place to type in that USA Hockey number.  
  5. Enter the number, and click Next and follow the instructions.  
  6. Once complete, we have captured all the USA Hockey information along with generating a 5 digit Season Pass code.  This is an important number.
  7. You will receive an e-mail with all this information, and you will want to print off a copy and save it for SCAHA tryouts.
That is it.   This SCAHA Season Pass Code will be used to identify your child at any SCAHA club tryout, and will be used to electronically sign any LOI that may be offered.   Treat it like a personal PIN.  It identifies your player(s) to the SCAHA system.
