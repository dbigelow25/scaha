The Southern California Amateur Hockey Association (SCAHA) is conducting the SCAHA Pre-Select Camp to select players from Southern California, whom are registered with USA Hockey, to attend the California Amateur Hockey Association (CAHA) Select Camp, to be held in January 2015.



This year's select camp will be held December 13th and 14th for players with a birth year of 1998, 1999, 2000, or 2001. Registration for the camp will be available on this site in October.
