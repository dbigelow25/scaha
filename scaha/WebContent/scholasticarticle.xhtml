Congratulations to Elizabeth Chapman, Jack Lee and Christopher Kang!





It is with pleasure and pride that SCAHA, the Anaheim Ducks and Los Angeles Kings have selected each of you as one of three recipients of the Season 2013-2014 SCAHA Scholastic Financial Assistance Awards! You have demonstrated excellence in your academic endeavors, as well as your participation as a hockey player. We want to recognize your accomplishments, and ask you to consider appearing at the June SCAHA Board meeting on Wednesday June 11, at The Rinks-Lakewood. In conjunction with the Anaheim Ducks and Los Angeles Kings, we will be presenting an award of $1,500 each for your benefit to help offset your education goal expenses.
